About Reality Shares

A firm exclusively focused on dividend growth

Access the future of dividend growth

At Reality Shares, we are solely focused on dividend growth investing. From our rigorous research process to the development of indices, dividend rankings and market indicators, everything we do is dedicated to analyzing and delivering new dividend growth opportunities to investors and their advisors.

Founded in 2012, Reality Shares began when a group of entrepreneurial industry veterans came together with a pioneering purpose: to take institutional-quality dividend growth strategies and deliver them to the masses. We recognized dividend growth as a means of cutting through the noise in the markets and investing in the true health of a company—investing in reality. Our team developed a series of powerful models that isolate, analyze, assess and predict dividend growth potential. Today, our rules-based, forward-looking methodology sets us apart in the market and through our ETFs, allows investors to access and harness the real power of dividend growth investing.

We offer a range of ETFs that pinpoint and capitalize on investment in dividend growth and the stocks that are most likely to increase their dividends, as well as avoiding those that are more likely to cut their dividends. Our proprietary DIVCON scoring system systematically ranks companies’ future dividend growth prospects based on a weight average of seven quality factors that are correlated to dividend growth.
